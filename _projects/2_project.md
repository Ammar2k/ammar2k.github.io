Built an app that turns uploaded images into narrated stories.

- Caption generation from user-uploaded images
- Story generation from the caption
- Text-to-speech conversion to audio
- [GitHub](https://github.com/Ammar2k/image_to_audio-story)

**Tech:** OpenAI API, HuggingFace, LangChain, Streamlit, TTS
